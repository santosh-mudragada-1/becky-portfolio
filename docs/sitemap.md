# Experience Concept

## The PM's Workspace

This portfolio is designed as the workspace of a Product Manager.

Instead of navigating through web pages, visitors explore the different objects on a creative product desk.

Every object tells part of the story.

A notebook introduces the person.

A whiteboard explains projects.

A dashboard shows analytics.

A coffee mug hides easter eggs.

A roadmap explains the journey.

The experience should feel natural, playful, and memorable while remaining easy to navigate.

# Hidden Moments

The workspace rewards exploration.

Examples

* Open drawers to discover hidden notes.
* Find doodles with funny comments.
* Click the coffee mug for product jokes.
* Reveal hidden sticky notes.
* Unlock achievement badges.
* Discover tiny interactions around the workspace.

These moments should delight visitors without distracting from the main story.

---

# Design Principles

The workspace should feel:

* Creative
* Warm
* Organized
* Playful
* Human
* Product-focused

Avoid making it feel like a game.

Avoid making it feel like a desktop operating system.

The workspace should support storytelling, not replace it.

---

# Success Criteria

Visitors should:

* Understand who I am within one minute.
* Enjoy exploring the workspace.
* Learn how I think through interaction.
* Easily discover my projects.
* Remember my personality.
* Leave wanting to start a conversation.
